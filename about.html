My story

from "10 Most Successful Lawyers of Moscow" to User Experience Designer.

I was born, raised, and lived most of my life in Moscow. Being a natural Moscow person means being quick in everything! In making decisions, in lifestyle, in your career. Moscow is a high-speed and dynamic city, and I love it. That's why when I came to New York, I fell in love with this city. New York has the same vibe plus unique energy and charm.

I practiced law for many years in Moscow before coming to the U.S. In 2010 I was one of the "10 Most Successful Lawyers in Moscow". But looking at my whole life story, I realized - I was ready to write a new chapter.

And a new story began. When researching a new career, I knew I wanted to leverage my passion and my experience. I have always loved the design, fashion, and art.

My "Aha" moment occurred when I realized that there is a career where I can combine my passion and my experience - this is UX Design.

The work of a lawyer involves the same process as a UX Design - "Double Diamond." The process of a lawyer's work is to: Discover (identify all pain points and problems of the case), Define (develop a strategy and methods), Design (create a case story), Deliver (to convince the court and judge and win :)

And... here I am!
